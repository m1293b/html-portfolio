My favourite movies of all time

Top 3 movies

Tenet

I really liked the heroism, especially at the end of the movie. The secrecy about the identity of the person who sacrifies themself to save the main characters, as in order to have the world saved, they needed to succeed.

Oppenheimer

The historic element of the movie was fairly new to me, as I haven't dug myself quite deep into the events of inventing the first Atomic Bomb. It was good to see what Oppenheimer went through, as before I saw this movie, I only, and barely, knew Oppenheimer as the father of the Atomic Bomb. This well presented story helped me get a better understanding of the power plays between the Soviet Union and the United States.

Inception

At the time, in 2010, I started to realise how interested I am in how the mind works. Inception helped me understand how important it is to be in control of one's mind, as it can play tricks on itself that can have devistated effects on other people. Especially on who we love the most.
